Privacy Policy for Android Share Extension
Effective Date: February 18, 2026

1. Overview
The "Android Share" extension (the "Add-on") is built on a foundational principle of Zero Data Collection. 
We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information, browsing history, or usage analytics. 
The Add-on is designed to operate strictly locally on your device.

2. Data Collection
We utilize a privacy-by-design approach.
- No Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any other personally identifiable 
information.
- No Tracking: We do not use cookies, pixels, or any third-party analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics) 
to track your behavior.
- No External Servers: The Add-on has no backend server. All processing—including URL sanitization and text extraction—occurs locally within your browser's memory.

3. Data Usage & Permissions
The Add-on requires specific permissions to function. Here is how they are used and why they are necessary:

- Access to All Websites (<all_urls> / host_permissions):
  Purpose: Required solely to inject the "Floating Action Button" (FAB) into the specific articles you visit.
  Usage: The Add-on only interacts with the page's DOM (structure) to read the title/URL upon your command. It does not monitor your browsing activity across the web.

- activeTab:
  Purpose: Used to retrieve the Title and URL of the current page when you initiate a share action.
  Usage: This data is temporarily held in memory to populate the share preview and is discarded immediately 
  after sharing.

- storage:
  Purpose: Used to save your local preferences (e.g., hiding specific share buttons, FAB position) 
  and your "Read Later" list.
  Usage: This data relies on browser.storage.local, which is a private storage area on your device 
  that is not synced to the cloud or accessible to us.

- scripting:
  Purpose: Used to execute the technical logic required for extracting text from the page.

4. Third-Party Services
The Add-on helps you share content to third-party platforms (e.g., X, WhatsApp, Bluesky).
- Link Sharing: When you choose to share a link, the Add-on constructs a sharing intent (URL) that opens t
he corresponding app or website on your device.
- Data Passthrough: The Add-on passes the sanitized URL and title directly to the target app via the 
Android system. We do not intermediate this connection or log what you share.
- Privacy policies of these third parties apply: Once you leave the Add-on to complete a share, 
your interactions are governed by the privacy policy of the destination app.

5. Changes to This Policy
If we make material changes to how we handle your data, we will update this policy and notify you via 
the Add-on's release notes. Given our commitment to user privacy, we do not anticipate changing our 
zero-collection stance.

6. Contact
If you have questions about this privacy policy or the Add-on's practices, please contact the developer 
via the support email listed on the Add-ons Manager page.
